FACILITIES

TRANSPORTATION

Our school provides a comprehensive transportation facility ensuring safe and convenient travel for students.

Equipped with GPS tracking, our buses are managed by experienced drivers and attendants to guarantee punctuality and security.

Routes are designed to cover extensive areas, ensuring that all students have easy access to school transportation. Regular maintenance and safety checks are conducted to ensure the highest standards of reliability and comfort.



LIBRARY

The library in our school stands as a treasure trove of knowledge, boasting an extensive collection of the best books across various genres and subjects.

From timeless classics to contemporary literature, our library caters to diverse interests and academic pursuits, ensuring that every student can find something captivating to read.

With cozy reading nooks, comfortable seating, and a tranquil atmosphere, the library provides the perfect environment for students to immerse themselves in the world of books and expand their horizons.



SMART CLASSROOMS

OUR SCHOOL has Spacious class rooms with all equipment.

Incorporating smart classrooms into our school environment revolutionizes the learning experience, seamlessly integrating technology to enhance teaching and student engagement.

Equipped with interactive whiteboards, audio-visual aids, and digital resources, these classrooms transform traditional lessons into dynamic, interactive sessions.

Teachers can utilize multimedia presentations, online resources, and educational apps to cater to diverse learning styles and captivate students' attention.



SCIENCE-LAB

The science lab at our school serves as a hub of exploration, experimentation, and discovery. Equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, safety measures, and ample workspace, it provides students with hands-on learning experiences that bring scientific principles to life.

From conducting experiments to analyzing data, students have the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge in a practical setting, fostering a deeper understanding of scientific concepts. Moreover, the science lab promotes curiosity, creativity, and a passion for inquiry, inspiring future scientists and innovators.



IN-DOOR GAMES

Incorporating indoor activities like chess, carrom, and abacus into school facilities offers numerous benefits for students. These traditional games and activities not only provide entertainment but also serve as valuable educational tools.

Chess, for instance, promotes critical thinking, strategy development, and concentration skills.

Carrom enhances hand-eye coordination, precision, and teamwork.

Abacus helps in arithmetic skills, mental calculation, and overall mathematical aptitude.



OUT-DOOR PLAY AREA

The very idea of including play, in required ratio with the academics, fulfills the prime objective of enriching students with creativity and thinking skills. To accomplish this, we at our SCHOOL allow students to explore different environments by providing them with indoor and outdoor sports arena.
